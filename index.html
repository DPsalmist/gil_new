About Us

Engineering Excellence You Can Rely On

Goodpalm International Limited (GIL) is a trusted engineering and power solutions company dedicated to keeping homes, industries, and businesses running without interruption. With over a decade of hands-on expertise, we specialize in the repair, overhaul, installation, and preventive maintenance of large industrial generators and mechanical systems. Our commitment is simple — delivering dependable, efficient, and long-lasting solutions tailored to our clients’ operational needs.

  • Expert repair & maintenance of large generators
  • Reliable electrical & mechanical engineering services
  • Professional logistics & equipment support services
  • Industry-standard training and capacity development
