Telecom Rectifier Services

Goodpalm International provides installation, maintenance and repair of telecom rectifier systems to ensure consistent DC power supply for telecom BTS, switching centers, and network infrastructure. Our services focus on reliability, redundancy and battery management.

Benefits of This Service

  • 1 Stable DC power for telecom equipment
  • 2 Improved battery health and backup reliability
  • 3 Reduced service interruptions for networks
  • 4 Expert site audits and optimization

We perform rectifier load balancing, battery testing, charger calibration, and preventive maintenance to minimize downtime and extend equipment life at critical telecom sites.

Why Choose GIL for Telecom Power?

With experience in telecom environments, GIL understands the critical requirements for uptime, redundancy and remote monitoring of rectifier systems.

Site Audits

Comprehensive checks to identify vulnerabilities and optimization opportunities.

Battery Management

Regular testing and replacement planning to avoid backup failures.

Remote Diagnostics

Support for rectifiers with telemetry and alarms for proactive maintenance.

Rapid Field Response

Fast dispatch to telecom sites for urgent rectifier faults.

  • Precautions
  • Maintenance Tasks
  • Telecom Focus

Safe battery handling, isolation of DC circuits, PPE and hot-swap protocols are enforced to protect staff and equipment during rectifier service.

Tasks: rectifier functional checks, charger calibration, battery capacity testing, load balancing and replacement planning.

We support telecom BTS sites, central offices, and power systems for carriers, with expertise in common telecom rectifier brands and configurations.
