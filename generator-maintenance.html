Generator Maintenance & Repairs

At GoodPalm International Ltd (GIL), we provide expert generator maintenance and repair services designed to keep your power systems running efficiently, safely, and without interruption. Whether for homes, businesses, telecom sites, or industrial facilities, our certified technicians deliver routine servicing, corrective repairs, and emergency support using the latest diagnostic tools and industry best practices.

Benefits of This Service

  • 1 Increased generator lifespan through routine preventive maintenance
  • 2 Reduced downtime and operational disruptions
  • 3 Improved fuel efficiency and optimal performance
  • 4 Early detection of faults to avoid costly damage

Our structured maintenance plans ensure your generator remains in peak condition all year round. From oil changes to load testing, our team applies a thorough, technical approach that guarantees reliability and consistency for your power supply.

We follow strict safety protocols including fuel handling procedures, electrical isolation, cooling system checks, fire risk control, and vibration assessments. Every maintenance activity is carried out to prevent hazards, equipment damage, and avoidable downtime.

Our maintenance workflow includes oil and filter replacement, coolant inspection, battery testing, alternator checks, AVR calibration, load testing, and general fault diagnosis. All service reports are documented and shared with clients for transparency.

GIL specializes in diesel and gas generators ranging from 5KVA to 1500KVA. We service brands such as Perkins, FG Wilson, Cummins, CAT, Mikano, JMG, and many more. Whether standby or prime power systems, our team delivers tailored maintenance solutions suited to your facility requirements.
