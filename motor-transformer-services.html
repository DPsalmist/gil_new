Motor & Transformer Services

Our motor and transformer services include testing, rewinding, refurbishment, installation and routine maintenance. We support operational reliability for industrial motors, distribution transformers, and critical electrical infrastructure.

Benefits of This Service

  • 1 Extended equipment life through professional servicing
  • 2 Improved efficiency and reduced energy losses
  • 3 Minimized risk of unexpected failures
  • 4 Expert on-site diagnostics & repair

Our workshop and field teams use precision testing tools to measure insulation resistance, winding integrity, and load performance — ensuring motors and transformers operate within safe parameters.

Why Choose GIL for Motor & Transformer Work?

We combine workshop capabilities with on-site service to provide full lifecycle support for rotating machines and transformers.

Workshop & Field Service

Repairs, rewinds, and testing in our workshop or on-site.

Testing Standards

Industry-standard testing for insulation, turns ratio and load performance.

Spare Parts

Access to quality replacement parts and rewinding materials.

Emergency Repair

Rapid response for critical motor or transformer faults.

  • Precautions
  • Service Steps
  • Equipment Types

Safety includes proper lifting techniques, isolation, hot-work controls, and handling of heavy components with certified lifting gear.

Typical steps: inspection → testing → diagnosis → repair/rewind → balance & alignment → final testing.

We service AC/DC motors, low-voltage and medium-voltage transformers, and offer rewinding and refurbishment.
